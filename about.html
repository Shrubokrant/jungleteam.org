One developer, a whole jungle of tools.

Jungle is my one-person studio: I design, build, document and market every plugin myself, from the design and engineering down to the marketing and customer support.

Every game I worked on needed the same things: clean editor tooling, design centric gameplay systems, little productivity boosters to make the day-to-day faster. So I kept building them. Over the years those tools grew up. My goal is to keep them performant, modular and genuinely pleasant to use. When they are good enough for others to use, I make them available!

That's what Jungle is. A family of tools, all sharing one modular framework with full source, built with both performance and good UX in mind.
